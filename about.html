About Me

I enjoyed playing video games very much, and I still am proud to say it. And my friends also enjoy playing as well. Eventually, we all had our curious brains create hypotheses about how much damage a weapon does, or just mess about what we can do to experiment with the game.

How did I become a "Gaming Scientist"?

It all started a few years ago, where I took two programming class in high school. After the first day, I kept writing simplistic version of code which led me to analyzing and experimenting around different games. Thus, the Gamer Critic was created.
